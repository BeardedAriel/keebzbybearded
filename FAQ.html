What is Keebs by Bearded?

Keebs by Bearded is a small, single-man operated company specializing in custom mechanical keyboards. We cater to customers who want high-quality, personalized keyboards tailored to their needs.

How long will it take for my order to ship?

Orders typically ship within 15- 25 business days. Custom builds or out-of-stock items may take up to 5 weeks. We’ll notify you of any delays.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping times and costs vary by location. Contact us for more details.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, we’ll send you a tracking number via email to monitor your shipment’s progress.

Are the prices listed inclusive of taxes?

No, prices do not include taxes. Sales tax is calculated at checkout based on your shipping address. Import duties or customs fees may apply for international orders.

What is your return policy?

Due to the custom nature of our products, returns are only accepted for damaged or defective items. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order to resolve any issues.

Can I cancel my order?

Contact us as soon as possible to cancel your order. If the order hasn’t shipped, we’ll cancel it. Once shipped, cancellations are not possible.

Can I customize my keyboard?

Yes! Customization is our specialty. We offer a variety of options for key switches, keycaps, layouts, and more. Please fill out our preferences form during checkout, and we'll ensure your keyboard is built according to your exact specifications.

Do you offer gift cards?

Currently, we do not offer gift cards. However, feel free to purchase a keyboard or accessory as a gift, and we’ll make sure it’s packaged beautifully for presentation.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely! We use secure payment processing methods and work with trusted partners to ensure your payment information is protected. We accept the following payment methods:
• Cash - 75% down payment pre Delivery
• PayPal
• BIT App (Israel clients only)
• PayBox (Israel clients only)

How can I contact you?

You can contact us via our Instgram page, or email us directly at beardedkeebz@tutamail.com. Our customer support team will get back to you as quickly as possible, usually within 1-2 business days.

Fine Print

• **Shipping Times**: We are a small, single-man company, and as such, we strive to offer the best service we can, but we may require additional time for certain custom orders. Please understand that shipping times may vary.
• **Taxes & Fees**: As mentioned earlier, sales tax and any applicable duties or import fees are the responsibility of the buyer. These will be calculated at checkout, and the final price will reflect these charges.
• **Customization**: While we pride ourselves on making custom keyboards, please note that some options may require longer build times due to parts availability or special customization requests.
• **Order Modifications**: If you need to change an order after placing it, please reach out as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request before the order is processed.

We appreciate your understanding and support. Thank you for choosing Keebs by Bearded for your custom mechanical keyboard needs!
